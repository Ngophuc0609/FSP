# PRODUCT VISION & PERSONAS MICRO-CONTEXT

## 1. Target Enterprise Market & Value Proposition
FSP is an **Enterprise Multi-Tenant SaaS Platform** targeting mid-to-large service organizations (HVAC contractors, industrial machinery manufacturers, medical equipment maintainers, telecommunications providers).
**Core Value Proposition:**
- Reduce technician travel time by 25% via smart routing.
- Increase first-time fix rate (FTFR) to over 85% by ensuring correct parts and checklists are available offline.
- Eliminate billing leakage and accelerate cash flow by capturing digital signatures and parts consumption instantly on site.

---

## 2. Core User Personas
1. **Bob (Field Service Technician):** Operates on mobile (Android/iOS) under harsh field conditions. Needs large buttons, high contrast, zero latency, and 100% offline reliability.
2. **Alice (Dispatcher / Operations Coordinator):** Operates on large desktop multi-monitor web browsers. Needs real-time map tracking, drag-and-drop scheduling board, and instant SLA warning indicators.
3. **David (Service Manager / Tenant Admin):** Operates on web dashboard. Needs executive KPI metrics (FTFR, MTTR, Technician Utilization), custom checklist builder, and billing reports.
4. **Elena (Customer Contact / Facility Manager):** Operates on self-service web portal or SMS tracking link. Needs transparent technician arrival ETA and instant access to service reports.
